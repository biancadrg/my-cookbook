snacks
Honey & Sriracha Hot Wings
Prep10 min + 2hr chill
Cook50 min
Servings4
DifficultyEasy

🪛 Equipment

Large shallow roasting tinBaking parchmentPastry brushLarge bowlSmall bowl

🍅 Ingredients

  • --- Wings ---
  • 16 chicken wings (~1.5kg)
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic granules
  • 2 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tsp sea salt flakes
  • Black pepper
  • 2 tsp sesame seeds (optional)
  • --- Glaze ---
  • 4 tbsp sriracha
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp cider vinegar

📝 Steps

  1. Mix the paprika, garlic granules, baking powder, cornflour, sea salt, and a good grinding of black pepper together. Tip the wings into a large bowl, scatter half the seasoning and toss. Add the rest and toss again until completely coated. Cover and chill for at least 2 hours, or up to 24 hours.
  2. Heat oven to 230°C / 210°C fan / gas 8. Line a large shallow roasting tin with baking parchment.
  3. Arrange wings in a single layer, fleshy-side down. Roast for 20 minutes, then flip and roast another 25-30 minutes until the skin is blistered and crisp.
  4. Whisk the glaze ingredients together in a small bowl until smooth.
  5. Remove wings from oven and brush liberally with the glaze. Scatter sesame seeds if using. Roast for another 8-10 minutes until the glaze is bubbling and starting to char.
  6. Serve straight from the tin or pile onto a plate.

🏷️ Dietary

dairy-free

💬 Notes

The baking powder is the secret to crispy skin without deep frying. Chill time makes a big difference so don't skip it.
